About Barrington Mobile Tire Pros

Your Local Experts in Mobile Tire Service

Serving Barrington & NW Suburbs for 10+ Years

At Barrington Mobile Tire Pros, we believe that getting new tires shouldn't be a hassle. For over a decade, we've been serving the Barrington community with a simple mission: bring the tire shop to the customer.

We understand that your time is valuable. Whether you're a busy professional working from home, a parent juggling school runs, or managing a fleet of vehicles, waiting at a tire shop is the last thing you want to do. That's why we invested in state-of-the-art mobile tire vans equipped with the latest mounting and balancing technology.

Why Choose Us?

  • Convenience: We come to your home, office, or roadside location.
  • Expertise: Our technicians are TIA-certified and experienced with all vehicle types, from sedans to luxury SUVs.
  • Fair Pricing: We operate with lower overhead than traditional shops, passing the savings on to you.
  • Safety: We use precision balancing and torque specifications to ensure your safety on the road.
