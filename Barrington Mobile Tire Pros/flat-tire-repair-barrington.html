Don't Wait for a Tow Truck

There is never a convenient time for a flat tire. Whether you're heading to work in the morning, picking up kids from Barrington High School, or stuck on Northwest Highway late at night, a flat tire can ruin your day. Traditional solutions involve waiting hours for a tow truck to drag your car to a shop that might not even be open.

Barrington Mobile Tire Pros offers a better way. Our fully equipped mobile tire vans come directly to your location—home, office, or roadside—to repair or replace your tire on the spot.

Our Flat Repair Services

Puncture Repair

We use permanent patch-plug repairs for nail and screw punctures in the tread area. This is the safest, industry-standard repair method.

Spare Tire Installation

If your tire is shredded or has sidewall damage, we'll safely install your spare tire to get you moving again.

Valve Stem Replacement

Leaking valve stems are a common cause of slow leaks. We can replace them quickly and re-pressurize your tire.

Rim Cleaning & Sealing

Corrosion on the rim bead can cause air loss. We clean and seal the bead area to ensure an airtight fit.
